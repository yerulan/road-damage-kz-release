## Reviewer Entry Notes

SUSY may ask for reviewer title, institution, email, and reason. A concise
reason can be:

> The candidate has published on road-damage detection datasets, geospatial
> infrastructure monitoring, or computer-vision methods for pavement distress,
> which is directly relevant to the submitted manuscript.

Do not suggest anyone from Al-Farabi Kazakh National University or any recent
coauthor/collaborator of the authors.
