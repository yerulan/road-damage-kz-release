# Cover Letter Draft

Dear Editors of Applied Sciences,

We are pleased to submit the manuscript entitled "Road Damage Detection and Classification for Kazakhstan Road Imagery" for consideration as an Article in Applied Sciences.

The manuscript studies automatic pavement-distress detection for Kazakhstan road imagery. It combines a provenance-audited Kazakhstan benchmark with an adapted Ghost-CA-YOLO detector that uses Ghost bottlenecks for lightweight feature generation and Coordinate Attention for directional crack sensitivity. The detector is trained on RDD2022, evaluated under Kazakhstan domain shift, and fine-tuned using the Kazakhstan subset. The work emphasizes both practical model efficiency and reproducible regional benchmarking.

The main contributions are:

1. A Kazakhstan road-damage benchmark with documented source, license, privacy, split, and annotation manifests.
2. An adapted Ghost-CA-YOLO architecture for efficient road-damage detection and classification.
3. A comparison with a YOLO11s baseline on RDD2022 and Kazakhstan imagery, including transfer and Kazakhstan fine-tuning experiments.
4. A reproducible release plan containing code, manifests, annotations, reports, and reconstruction instructions without redistributing third-party raw images by default.

The manuscript is original, has not been published elsewhere, and is not under consideration by another journal. All authors will approve the final submitted version. The research received no external funding. Conflicts of interest, author contributions, and data availability statements are included in the manuscript.
