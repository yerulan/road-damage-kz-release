## Research Gap

Existing road-damage datasets are geographically broad but do not provide a Kazakhstan-specific benchmark with explicit license auditing and privacy review. This project should avoid overstating novelty: the likely contribution is not a new detection architecture, but a reproducible Kazakhstan-focused evaluation pipeline and legally reusable dataset subset.

## Claims To Avoid Until Proven

- "first Kazakhstan road damage dataset";
- "large-scale dataset";
- "state-of-the-art model";
- "representative of all Kazakhstan roads";
- "automatic severity assessment."

## Claims That May Be Defensible

- "Kazakhstan-focused";
- "license-audited";
- "reproducible benchmark";
- "baseline domain-shift evaluation";
- "internet-sourced imagery with documented reuse rights."
